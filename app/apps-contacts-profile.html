About
Bio :

Bob Gilbert is the Vice President of Security Cloud GTM Strategy and Chief Evangelist at Netskope. In his role, he is focused on helping clients transform their security and networking infrastructure to meet the challenges of a rapidly evolving digital world. His expertise lies in Security Service Edge (SSE) and Secure Access Service Edge (SASE), as well as Zero Trust frameworks, where he provides expert guidance and innovative solutions.

Gilbert has a career spanning over 25 years in Silicon Valley, where he has held various strategic leadership roles in product marketing and management. Before joining Netskope, he served as Chief Evangelist at Riverbed Technology, contributing significantly to the company's growth from a startup to a market leader in WAN optimization.

He has been a prominent speaker and presenter, engaging with live audiences in over 55 countries. Gilbert is also passionate about education, teaching cybersecurity courses at ESADE's International Business School and serving as a guest lecturer at the University of San Francisco's MBA program for six years.

His interest in cybersecurity began in the 1980s when he developed a firewall tool to protect his Bulletin Board System (BBS), igniting a lifelong passion for digital security and shaping his career in the cybersecurity industry
